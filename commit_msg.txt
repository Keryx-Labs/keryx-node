consensus: implement OPoI Phase 2 real escrow split in coinbase

Each blue-block coinbase is now split three ways on the gross reward: 
75% -> miner's script_public_key                                        
20% -> escrow SPK (OPoI miner) or provably-unspendable burn address     
5% -> R&D allocation 

OPoI miners signal participation by embedding /escrow:<64-hex-pubkey>
in their coinbase extra_data. The new parse_escrow_from_extra_data                               
method on CoinbaseManager parses this and routes the 20% to a real, 
recoverable P2PK output. Standard miners have their 20% burned to
TransactionHash("KERYX_PROOF_OF_BURN_V1") — not a valid EC point,
permanently removing coins from supply.                                                                                    
The red-block reward is excluded from the escrow split to keep modify_block_template 
compatible (single output rewrite via red_reward_output_index)
